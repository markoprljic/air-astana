Batumi: Where the Mountains Meet the Sea
Batumi is Georgia's most cosmopolitan seaside city — a place where Soviet-era boulevards meet gleaming towers, ancient churches face modern sculpture, and the Black Sea laps at a broad pebbly shore backed by the Caucasus mountains.
Whether you're drawn by the architecture, the food, the nightlife, or simply the warm waters, Batumi rewards the curious traveller at every turn.
